About

Commercial Pilot | CFII | Open to Relocation

In 2020, my life took a sharp turn in the best possible way. My family relocated from Middletown, Maryland to St. Thomas in the U.S. Virgin Islands, a move that opened the door to experiences that would shape who I am today.

I threw myself into island life, learning to sail, building lifelong friendships, and developing a deep connection to a place where the sea and sky feel limitless. It was here that my interest in aviation truly took hold.

At Virgin Islands Montessori School & Peter Gruber International Academy, one of the Caribbean's only International Baccalaureate schools, I balanced the demands of a full IB curriculum with varsity soccer, earning my Eagle Scout, and working part time at The Smoking Rooster, all while beginning my flight training.

At 16, I was awarded the highly competitive Coral Wings Aviation Scholarship through the University of the Virgin Islands and the FAA. That moment turned a growing passion into a clear career path.

Today, I am training full time in the Airline Academy Program at American Flyers in Florida. Since September 2025, I have earned my Private Pilot License and Instrument Rating, and I am on track to complete my Commercial License, CFI, CFII, and MEI by June 26, 2026. Upon graduation, I will have over 200 hours of Pilot in Command time, with an even greater drive to continue learning and progressing in aviation.

I am actively seeking my first commercial opportunity and am eager to contribute, learn, and grow in any environment that challenges me.

Certifications

Six FAA Ratings

Two earned. Four in progress, on track to complete the full Airline Academy Program by June 26, 2026.

PPL

Private Pilot

The foundation of all flight training. Authorizes solo and cross-country flight as pilot-in-command under visual flight rules, including night operations and basic instrument reference.

Completed December 2025

IFR

Instrument Rating

Authorizes flight in instrument meteorological conditions and operations under IFR. Covers precision approaches, holding patterns, and full situational awareness through the cockpit instruments alone.

Completed January 2026

CPL

Commercial Pilot

Authorizes compensation for flight operations. Builds on private and instrument foundations with higher precision standards, complex aircraft endorsements, and commercial maneuver proficiency.

In progress — target April 2026

CFI

Flight Instructor

Authorizes the holder to provide flight instruction to student pilots seeking private and commercial certificates. Requires mastery of teaching techniques, aeronautical knowledge, and airmanship.

In progress — target June 2026

CFII

Instrument Instructor

Authorizes instruction in instrument flight rules and procedures. Combines the full instrument rating curriculum with the teaching skills required to develop safe and proficient IFR pilots.

In progress — target June 2026

ME

Multi Engine

Authorizes operation of a twin-engine aircraft. Includes critical engine failure management, asymmetric thrust procedures, and the full systems knowledge required for multi-engine operations.

In progress — target June 2026
